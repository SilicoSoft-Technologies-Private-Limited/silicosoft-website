Product 01 / Healthcare

SiliMed HIMS

Hospital Information Management System

A complete hospital operating system. Registration to discharge, laboratory to pharmacy, ward to billing — one integrated platform built around how clinicians actually work. ABDM-ready, insurance-integrated, and proven in multi-specialty environments.

◆ MOD—01

OPD & registration

Queue management, token generation, doctor consultation notes, prescription printing with digital signatures. Walk-ins and appointments handled in one flow.

◆ MOD—02

IPD & ward management

Admission to discharge, bed allocation, nursing station dashboards, doctor's orders, treatment sheets and clinical notes — fully electronic, printable when required.

◆ MOD—03

Laboratory (LIS)

Sample collection, bidirectional analyzer interface, quality control, report generation with reference ranges, doctor's review and NABL-compliant templates.

◆ MOD—04

Pharmacy & inventory

Batch-wise stock, expiry alerts, purchase orders, GRN, issue to wards, retail sales, Schedule H tracking — with automatic stock deduction on IPD consumption.

◆ MOD—05

Billing & TPA

Consolidated bills across OPD, IPD, lab and pharmacy. Insurance claims with TPA integration, package billing, corporate accounts and daily revenue reconciliation.

◆ MOD—06

EMR & ABDM

Electronic medical records with ABHA linkage, DigiLocker health records, structured history, investigations, prescriptions and discharge summaries in FHIR format.

◆ MOD—07

Radiology & PACS

Modality worklist, DICOM viewer integration, reporting templates and image archival — works with CR, DR, CT, MRI and ultrasound systems.

◆ MOD—08

Analytics & MIS

Daily census, departmental revenue, drug consumption trends, doctor performance, TAT dashboards and board-ready MIS packs — delivered on schedule.

Product 02 / Education

SiliCampus CMS

College Management System

A campus-wide backbone. From the first online application to the final degree, SiliCampus carries the entire academic lifecycle — with modules for teachers, students, parents, administrators and accountants that actually feel built for each one.

◆ MOD—01

Admissions & enquiry

Online application forms, document upload, entrance scorecard import, merit list generation, counselling sessions and fee payment in a single workflow.

◆ MOD—02

Examination & results

Exam scheduling, internal marks, descriptive and MCQ assessments, tabulation register, provisional results, grade cards and convocation-ready transcripts.

◆ MOD—03

Attendance

Biometric and RFID integrations, period-wise marking from mobile, parent SMS alerts on absence, subject-wise shortage reports and condonation tracking.

◆ MOD—04

Fees & finance

Online payment gateway, installment plans, scholarship adjustments, late-fee logic, refund workflow, 26AS-ready receipts and day-book reconciliation.

◆ MOD—05

Library & resources

Catalogue, member management, issue and return with RFID or barcode, overdue tracking, acquisition budgeting and OPAC for students.

◆ MOD—06

Hostel & transport

Room allocation, mess accounting, visitor logs, fleet routes, driver assignments, GPS live tracking and parent notifications on arrival and departure.

◆ MOD—07

LMS & assignments

Course materials, assignment submission, plagiarism flagging, teacher feedback, live classrooms via Zoom or Meet and student performance analytics.

◆ MOD—08

NAAC & NIRF reports

Accreditation-ready data packs, AQAR inputs, NIRF submission sheets, placement dashboards and outcome-based education tracking aligned to UGC norms.

Product 03 / Enterprise HR

SiliPay HRMS

Payroll & Human Resources

A payroll engine built for Indian compliance. PF, ESI, PT, TDS, LWF, gratuity, bonus — all current with statutory change, all auditable, all generated without a spreadsheet in sight. Scales from a 50-person office to a 5,000-person enterprise.

◆ MOD—01

Employee master

Full record lifecycle from offer letter to full-and-final settlement, with document vault, KYC verification, dependent tracking and career history.

◆ MOD—02

Attendance & leaves

Biometric and geo-fenced mobile punch, shift rosters, overtime calculation, multiple leave policies, accrual rules and multi-level approval chains.

◆ MOD—03

Payroll run

Attendance-to-salary automation with multiple pay components, loans, advances, arrears, reimbursements and configurable CTC structures.

◆ MOD—04

Statutory compliance

PF ECR, ESI challan, PT state-wise, TDS Form 16/24Q, LWF returns and gratuity provisioning — all kept current by our compliance desk.

◆ MOD—05

Self-service portal

Employees view payslips, file leave requests, claim reimbursements, upload tax-saving proofs and download Form 16 — from web and mobile app.

◆ MOD—06

Performance & exit

Goal setting, 360° reviews, appraisal letters, resignation workflow, clearance routing and automated full-and-final calculation with exit analytics.

◆ MOD—07

Reports & audit

Salary registers, cost-center breakdowns, department budgets, variance analysis, audit trails and board-ready packs generated on a schedule.

◆ MOD—08

Integrations

Tally, SAP, Oracle, biometric devices, bank statement uploads, direct salary transfer via corporate banking APIs and SMS/email gateways.
