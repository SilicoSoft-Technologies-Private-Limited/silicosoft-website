§ 02 — Method

A practice, not a
project pipeline.

We work in small, senior teams. The same engineers who scope your system write its first line and sit in the room on go-live day. Continuity is the point.

STEP ⋅ 01

Listen

We walk your floors. Talk to the nurse at registration, the hostel warden, the accountant closing books. Requirements come from there — not a template.

STEP ⋅ 02

Draft

Information architecture, workflow maps, data models. We put a design document in your hands before a single line of production code.

STEP ⋅ 03

Build

Short cycles, working software in front of real users every two weeks. The scope stays honest because the feedback arrives quickly.

STEP ⋅ 04

Stay

Go-live is the beginning, not the finish line. On-site support, monthly releases, monitored uptime — we stay on the hook for as long as you need us.

The best software is the least noticed. It simply holds, so the work above it can happen.
— Team charter, SilicoSoft Technologies
