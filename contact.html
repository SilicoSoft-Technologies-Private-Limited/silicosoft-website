Quick answers

Before you write.

Q.01

How quickly can we start?

Discovery usually begins within 10 days of the first call. Engineering work varies by scope but we aim for a first working prototype inside six weeks.

Q.02

Do you work outside Jharkhand?

Our studio is in Ranchi; our deployments are across twelve states. For hospital and campus work, we fly in for go-live and spend time on site.

Q.03

What's a typical budget?

Small campus deployments start around ₹6 lakh one-time plus AMC. Multi-specialty HIMS engagements are meaningfully larger. We quote honestly after discovery.

Q.04

Do you build on-premise?

Yes — fully on-premise, hybrid, or cloud. We deploy wherever your compliance posture requires, and we document the architecture for your team.

Q.05

What about data ownership?

Your data is yours. Always. We sign data-processing agreements, maintain Indian data residency, and never use customer data in model training.

Q.06

Can we migrate from another vendor?

Yes — we've done this many times. We handle data extraction, cleansing, validation and parallel-run periods so your operations keep running through the switch.
